About MOBALE

Our Mission

The Maine Organization for Blind Athletic and Leadership Education (MOBALE) is dedicated to promoting sports and leadership opportunities for visually impaired youth throughout Maine and the surrounding region. Through our annual Summer Sports Education Camp and year-round programs, we empower young athletes to develop skills, confidence, and leadership abilities that extend far beyond the playing field.

What We Do

MOBALE provides comprehensive sports education and leadership development programs specifically designed for blind and visually impaired youth. Our programs include:

  • Summer Sports Education Camp: Our flagship annual program bringing together athletes for intensive multi-sport training
  • Leadership Development: Workshops and mentorship programs fostering personal growth and leadership skills
  • Adaptive Sports Training: Professional coaching in multiple sports adapted for visually impaired athletes
  • Community Building: Creating lasting connections between athletes, families, volunteers, and mentors
  • Advocacy: Promoting awareness and opportunities for visually impaired athletes in Maine and beyond

Our Impact

Since our founding, MOBALE has served dozens of visually impaired youth from across Maine and New Hampshire, providing life-changing experiences through sports and leadership education. Our participants have:

  • Developed confidence and independence through athletic achievement
  • Built lasting friendships with peers who share similar experiences
  • Learned leadership skills that carry into their academic and personal lives
  • Discovered new sports and activities they continue to pursue
  • Connected with mentors and role models in the blind and visually impaired community

Our Team

MOBALE is powered by a dedicated team of volunteers, coaches, counselors, and community partners who share our passion for empowering visually impaired youth. Our team includes:

  • Volunteer Counselors: Experienced mentors who provide guidance and support throughout camp
  • Visually Impaired Counselors: Role models who share their experiences and inspire our athletes
  • Junior Counselors: Former participants who return to support the next generation of athletes
  • Professional Coaches: Certified instructors with expertise in adaptive sports
  • Guest Speakers: Paralympic athletes, community leaders, and inspiring individuals

Partners & Supporters

MOBALE's success is made possible through partnerships with organizations dedicated to serving the blind and visually impaired community, including:

  • Alfond Youth & Community Center (2025 Organization of the Year)
  • Local schools and educational institutions
  • Paralympic sports organizations
  • Community recreation centers
  • Individual volunteers and donors

Get Involved

There are many ways to support MOBALE's mission:

  • Athletes: If you're a visually impaired youth interested in sports and leadership, we'd love to have you join us!
  • Volunteers: We always need dedicated volunteers to serve as counselors, coaches, and support staff
  • Donors: Financial support helps us keep programs accessible and expand our offerings
  • Partners: Organizations interested in supporting visually impaired athletes can connect with us
