Our Sports Programs

MOBALE offers a diverse range of adaptive sports designed specifically for blind and visually impaired athletes. Our programs emphasize skill development, safety, and fun while building confidence and independence.

Goalball

Goalball is a Paralympic team sport designed specifically for blind and visually impaired athletes. Three players per team defend their goal while trying to roll a ball with bells past the opposing team.

What You'll Learn:

  • Spatial awareness and orientation
  • Quick reflexes and defensive positioning
  • Team communication and strategy
  • Throwing accuracy and ball control

Accessibility Features:

  • All players wear eyeshades for equal competition
  • Ball contains bells for auditory tracking
  • Tactile lines mark court boundaries
  • Quiet environment required during play

Wrestling

Wrestling is an excellent sport for visually impaired athletes because physical contact maintains constant awareness of opponent position. Our program teaches fundamental techniques in a safe, supportive environment.

What You'll Learn:

  • Fundamental wrestling techniques and holds
  • Strength and conditioning
  • Balance and body control
  • Confidence and determination

Program Features:

  • Certified coaches with adaptive sports experience
  • Safety equipment provided
  • Modified rules when needed
  • Progressive skill development

Swimming

Swimming provides excellent cardiovascular exercise and is a crucial life skill. Our program adapts instruction for visually impaired swimmers in a safe pool environment.

What You'll Learn:

  • Swimming strokes and techniques
  • Water safety and confidence
  • Diving and starts (with adaptations)
  • Underwater navigation

Accessibility Features:

  • Tap system for wall approach warning
  • Verbal lane guidance
  • Qualified lifeguards and instructors
  • Pool familiarization and orientation

Track & Field

Track & Field offers diverse athletic opportunities from running to throwing events, all adapted for visually impaired athletes.

Events We Offer:

  • Sprints (with guide runners or guide wires)
  • Long-distance running
  • Shot put and discus
  • Long jump

Accessibility Features:

  • Guide runners with tethers for running events
  • Tactile markers for throwing circles
  • Acoustic signals for direction
  • Experienced coaches familiar with Paralympic standards

Disc Golf

Disc Golf combines outdoor recreation with skill development. This low-impact sport is accessible and enjoyable for athletes of all skill levels.

What You'll Learn:

  • Throwing techniques and disc selection
  • Course navigation with sighted guides
  • Distance and accuracy
  • Outdoor recreation and nature appreciation

Program Features:

  • Sighted guides for each athlete
  • Verbal descriptions of course layout
  • Practice range before course play
  • Friendly competitions and tournaments

Archery

Archery develops focus, precision, and upper body strength. With proper adaptations, blind and visually impaired athletes can excel in this Paralympic sport.

What You'll Learn:

  • Proper form and stance
  • Bow handling and safety
  • Aiming techniques adapted for visual impairment
  • Concentration and mental discipline

Accessibility Features:

  • Tactile aiming devices and sights
  • Spotters provide verbal feedback
  • Assistive equipment as needed
  • Safety protocols and supervision

Rock Climbing

Rock Climbing builds problem-solving skills, strength, and confidence. Our program uses indoor climbing walls with experienced instructors.

What You'll Learn:

  • Climbing techniques and holds
  • Problem-solving and route planning
  • Upper body and core strength
  • Trust and communication with belayers

Accessibility Features:

  • Verbal route descriptions
  • Tactile exploration of wall and holds
  • Certified climbing instructors
  • All safety equipment provided

Tandem Biking

Tandem Biking allows visually impaired athletes to experience the freedom and exhilaration of cycling with trained sighted partners (pilots).

What You'll Experience:

  • Cardiovascular exercise and endurance
  • Teamwork and communication
  • Outdoor exploration
  • Speed and freedom of cycling

Program Features:

  • Experienced pilots (front riders)
  • Quality tandem bicycles
  • Safe, scenic routes
  • Helmets and safety equipment provided

Soccer & Kickball

Soccer and Kickball are team sports that foster cooperation, cardiovascular fitness, and fun. We use balls with bells and verbal cues for successful play.

What You'll Learn:

  • Ball tracking and control
  • Team positioning and strategy
  • Communication on the field
  • Sportsmanship and teamwork

Accessibility Features:

  • Balls with bells or sound devices
  • Smaller field sizes when appropriate
  • Modified rules for fair play
  • Verbal coaching and guidance

Yoga & Functional Fitness

Yoga and Functional Fitness round out our program with flexibility, strength, and mindfulness training that supports all other athletic activities.

What You'll Learn:

  • Yoga poses and sequences
  • Breathing techniques and mindfulness
  • Core strength and stability
  • Injury prevention and recovery

Program Features:

  • Verbal cuing for all poses and movements
  • Tactile demonstrations when needed
  • Modifications for all ability levels
  • Focus on body awareness and control

Try These Sports at Camp!

All of these sports are offered at our annual Summer Sports Education Camp. You'll have the opportunity to try multiple sports, discover new interests, and develop skills in a supportive environment.
