Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MOBALE's programs, participation, and volunteering opportunities.

About Participation

Who is eligible to participate in MOBALE programs?

MOBALE serves blind and visually impaired youth primarily from Maine, though we welcome athletes from surrounding states. Age ranges and specific eligibility details vary by program - contact us for more information.

How much does it cost to attend camp?

We strive to keep our programs accessible and offer financial assistance when needed. Contact us to discuss fees and available scholarships.

When is the next Summer Sports Education Camp?

Our camp typically takes place in June each year. Registration information is usually available in early spring. Contact us to be added to our mailing list for updates.

Can family members attend camp?

Our camp is designed as a residential experience for athletes to build independence and peer connections. Family involvement varies by program - contact us for specific details.

What if my child has never played sports before?

That's perfectly fine! MOBALE welcomes athletes of all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced competitors. Our program focuses on skill development, confidence building, and having fun.

About Volunteering

Do volunteers need experience working with visually impaired individuals?

No previous experience is required! We provide training and support for all volunteers. What matters most is enthusiasm, patience, and a commitment to MOBALE's mission.

What volunteer opportunities are available?

We have various volunteer roles including camp counselors, sports instructors, junior counselors (for returning athletes), and support staff for logistics and operations.

How can I become a volunteer?

Use the contact form on our Get Involved page to express your interest. We'll provide information about upcoming opportunities, requirements, and training.

Still Have Questions?

If you don't see your question answered here, please reach out to us! We're happy to provide more information about our programs, participation, volunteering, or partnership opportunities.
