2024 Summer Sports Camp Photos

Posted on February 26, 2025

Take a look back at the amazing moments from our 2024 Summer Sports Camp! These photos capture the energy, determination, and joy of our athletes as they participated in various sports throughout the week.

[Photo Gallery: Athletes participating in goalball, diving for the ball and defending their goal]

Goalball

Goalball was a huge hit at the 2024 camp! Athletes learned the fundamentals of this Paralympic team sport, from defensive positioning to accurate throwing. The intensity and focus required for goalball helped athletes develop spatial awareness and quick reflexes.

[Photo Gallery: Wrestling instruction and matches with coaches and athletes on the mats]

Wrestling

Our wrestling program builds strength, technique, and confidence. Athletes worked with experienced coaches to learn proper form, practice moves, and participate in friendly matches. The one-on-one nature of wrestling, combined with constant physical contact, makes it an ideal sport for visually impaired athletes.

[Photo Gallery: Disc golf on the course, athletes throwing discs with guides providing direction]

Disc Golf

The outdoor disc golf course provided a beautiful setting for athletes to practice their throwing skills and enjoy nature. Working with sighted guides, athletes navigated the course and competed in a friendly tournament that tested their accuracy and distance control.

[Photo Gallery: Rock climbing wall activities with harnesses and spotters]

Rock Climbing

Rock climbing challenged our athletes both physically and mentally. With verbal route descriptions and hands-on exploration of the wall, athletes problem-solved their way to the top. The trust built between climbers and belayers was a powerful part of the experience.

[Photo Gallery: Tandem biking on trails with pilots and athletes enjoying the ride]

Tandem Biking

Tandem biking gave athletes the thrill of cycling through scenic routes. Paired with experienced pilots (front riders), athletes pedaled together and enjoyed the freedom and speed of biking. The teamwork and communication required made tandem biking a favorite activity.

Thank You!

Thank you to all the athletes, families, volunteers, coaches, and supporters who made the 2024 camp possible. We can't wait to create more amazing memories in 2025 and beyond!
