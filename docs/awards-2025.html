2025 Summer Sports Camp Award Winners

Posted on July 20, 2025

At the conclusion of our 2025 Summer Sports Education Camp, we were thrilled to recognize the outstanding achievements of our athletes, volunteers, and partners. Congratulations to all our award winners!

Disc Golf Tournament Results

Our annual disc golf tournament showcased the skills our athletes developed throughout the week. Competing in teams with sighted guides, athletes navigated the course with accuracy and determination.

Tournament Winners:

Spirit Award

Delaney K. earned the Spirit Award for her positive attitude, encouragement of fellow athletes, and embodiment of MOBALE's values. Delaney's enthusiasm was contagious, and she made everyone around her feel welcome and supported.

Athlete of the Year

Will H. was named 2025 Athlete of the Year for his exceptional athletic performance, dedication, leadership, and sportsmanship. Will participated fully in every activity, pushed himself to improve, and served as an inspiring example to his peers. His commitment to excellence and willingness to try new challenges made him a standout athlete this year.

Volunteer of the Year

Cheryl G. received the Volunteer of the Year award in recognition of her tireless dedication, patience, and passion for MOBALE's mission. Cheryl went above and beyond in every aspect of camp, from leading activities to providing emotional support to athletes. Her positive energy and commitment made a lasting impact on everyone at camp.

Organization of the Year

The Alfond Youth & Community Center was honored as Organization of the Year for their outstanding partnership and support of MOBALE's programs. The Alfond Center has provided facilities, resources, and collaborative opportunities that have been invaluable to our success.

Celebrating Everyone

While these formal awards recognize specific achievements, every athlete, volunteer, and supporter who participated in camp deserves recognition. Each person contributed to the welcoming, challenging, and fun environment that makes MOBALE special. Thank you all for an amazing 2025 camp!
