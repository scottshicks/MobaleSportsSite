MOBALE Sports Education Camp 2022

December 23, 2022

The 2022 camp was held at the Alfond Youth Center with 12 athletes, including 2 junior counselors and 7 volunteers. Activities included track and field, goalball, dance, swimming, and rock climbing. Sadie Hammond was named Volunteer of the Year for teaching accessible tennis.

August 8, 2020

Due to COVID-19, MOBALE held a virtual sports camp via Zoom with six athletes. Activities included strengthening exercises, yoga, and karate. Sydney Mason was named Volunteer of the Year for her martial arts instruction during this challenging time.
