Get Involved with MOBALE

There are many ways to support MOBALE's mission of empowering visually impaired youth through sports and leadership education. Whether you're an athlete looking to participate, a volunteer wanting to give back, or a supporter interested in helping, we'd love to hear from you!

Ways to Get Involved

Athletes

Are you a blind or visually impaired youth interested in trying new sports and developing your athletic skills? Our Summer Sports Education Camp welcomes athletes from Maine and surrounding states.

What to expect:

  • Multi-day residential camp experience
  • 10+ different sports and activities
  • Professional coaching and instruction
  • Leadership development workshops
  • Peer connections and mentorship
  • Fun, supportive, and challenging environment

Use the contact form below to learn more about registration, eligibility, and what to expect.

Volunteers

MOBALE's success depends on dedicated volunteers who share our passion for empowering visually impaired youth. No previous experience working with visually impaired individuals is required!

Volunteer opportunities:

  • Camp Counselors - Provide supervision, mentorship, and support to athletes
  • Sports Instructors - Lead specific sport activities and coaching
  • Junior Counselors - Former participants who mentor new athletes
  • Support Staff - Help with logistics, meals, and operations

Contact us to learn more about volunteer opportunities and requirements.

Donors & Supporters

Financial support helps MOBALE keep programs accessible and expand our offerings. Your donation makes a direct impact on the lives of visually impaired youth.

Your support provides:

  • Scholarships for athletes who need financial assistance
  • Sports equipment and adaptive gear
  • Facility rentals and transportation
  • Professional coaching and instruction
  • Camp supplies and materials

Contact us to discuss donation opportunities and how your support can make a difference.

Organizations & Partners

MOBALE collaborates with schools, recreation centers, blindness organizations, and community groups to expand opportunities for visually impaired youth.

Partnership opportunities:

  • Facility and equipment sharing
  • Athlete recruitment and outreach
  • Volunteer coordination
  • Financial and in-kind support
  • Program development and collaboration

Let's work together to create more opportunities for visually impaired athletes!
